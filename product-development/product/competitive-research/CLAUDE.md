## Berlitz Competitive Positioning

| Dimension | Berlitz Advantage |
|-----------|-------------------|
| AI + Human Hybrid | Only platform combining unlimited AI practice with credit-gated human teachers at scale |
| Enterprise Depth | Admin-managed credit pools, ROI dashboards, HRIS integration, pre/post assessments |
| Instructor Quality | 97.8% satisfaction, 5-year avg tenure, structured QA -- not a marketplace |
| Outcome Certification | CEFR-aligned assessments with verifiable credentials tied to business impact |
| Content Breadth | Language + Skills + Culture programs (compliance, negotiation, cross-cultural) |
| Usage-Based Monetization | Credit system enables AI/human blending; scales from free to enterprise |

## Key Takeaways

1. No competitor combines AI avatar practice, human coaching, and enterprise outcomes in one platform
2. AI-native competitors (Speak, Praktika, TalkPal) lack human teachers and enterprise features
3. Enterprise competitors (Rosetta Stone, EF) have outdated AI and are displacement opportunities
4. Duolingo's free tier and gamification set the consumer engagement bar but lack depth and B2B

## Research Standards

All competitive research follows the standards in `.claude/agents/AGENTS.md` (Research Standards section). Key rules:

- Every claim cites its source inline with a URL.
- Key findings are verified by multiple independent sources where possible.
- Findings are tagged High/Medium/Low confidence (High = 2+ independent sources or primary source; Medium = 1 credible source; Low = unverified or estimated).
- Each research doc includes a Sources section, confidence summary, and methodology note.

## When to Update

- After competitive deal wins/losses
- When competitors launch new features
- After sales calls where competitors are mentioned
- Quarterly review of pricing and positioning
- After each competitor profile update (update `competitors/competitive-matrix.md`)
