# Italki - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Marketplace/pay-per-session. No subscription required for lessons (pay per session to individual tutors). Optional italki Plus membership for supplementary features. italki takes a 15% commission on each lesson from the teacher's earnings. [italki Commission Support](https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/206352068-How-does-italki-charge-a-commission), [Preply vs italki Commission Comparison](https://www.tutorlingua.co/blog/preply-vs-italki-vs-verbling-commission-comparison-2025)

## Enterprise/B2B Pricing

## Government/Institutional Pricing

Not available. [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price)

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays ~$20-60/month for 4 community tutor sessions ($5-15 each), optionally plus $5.99/mo for italki Plus features. [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price)
- **A serious learner** pays ~$80-320/month for 8 professional teacher sessions ($10-40 each). [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price)
- **An enterprise buyer** cannot purchase italki. No team management or admin features. [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price)

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | italki | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Free tier | Browse + book (pay per session) | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry cost (4 sessions/mo) | ~$20-60/mo (community tutors) [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price) | $19/mo (Plus, 2 AI tutor sessions) |
| Mid-tier (8 sessions/mo) | ~$80-200/mo (professional teachers) [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price) | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits incl. human) |
| Premium (16 sessions/mo) | ~$160-640/mo [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price) | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | Not available | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Not available (italki Plus has basic AI roleplay for $5.99/mo) [italki Plus](https://support.italki.com/hc/en-us/articles/37624223919385-What-is-italki-Plus) | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | $5-80/session (marketplace) [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price) | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Teacher quality | Variable (marketplace, reviews-based) [italki Review](https://todaytesting.com/italki-review/) | Curated (97.8% satisfaction) |
| Curriculum | None (tutor decides) [italki Review](https://todaytesting.com/italki-review/) | Structured CEFR-aligned |
| Languages | 150+ [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price) | Multiple (TBD count) |
| CEFR certification | No [italki Pricing Blog](https://www.italki.com/en/blog/italki-price) | Included |

**Key Insight:** italki's community tutors can be cheaper per session ($5-15) than Berlitz credits ($5-10), but without AI practice, learners need more paid sessions to achieve the same practice volume. A learner doing 8 professional sessions/month on italki ($80-200) pays more than Berlitz Pro ($49) while getting no AI practice, no curriculum, and no certification. Berlitz's hybrid model delivers more practice hours for less money.

## Methodology

Pricing data was collected on 2026-06-05 from italki's official website, support pages, and blog posts (primary sources), supplemented by third-party review sites. Tutor rate ranges reflect platform minimums ($5 for community tutors, $10 for professional teachers) as stated in italki's help center, with upper bounds based on italki's own pricing guide. The 15% commission rate was confirmed via italki's support page and cross-referenced with an independent platform comparison. Berlitz (Target) pricing in the comparison table reflects internal target pricing, not italki-sourced data.
