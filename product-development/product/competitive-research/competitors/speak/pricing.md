# Speak - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Freemium with subscription. Free tier with limited daily access; two paid tiers (Premium and Premium Plus) with monthly and annual billing ([Tekpon](https://tekpon.com/software/speak/pricing/); [SpeakShark](https://speakshark.com/blog/speak-app-pricing-per-month-2026)).

## Consumer Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price (per month) | What's Included |
|------|--------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | Limited daily lessons and conversations ([Practice Me](https://practiceme.app/vs/speak)) |
| Premium (monthly) | $20/mo | -- | Full lesson library, AI conversations, pronunciation feedback ([SpeakShark](https://speakshark.com/blog/speak-app-pricing-per-month-2026)) |
| Premium (annual) | -- | ~$8.25/mo ($99/yr) | Same as monthly, ~59% savings ([SpeakCoupon](https://www.speakcoupon.com/en); [SpeakShark](https://speakshark.com/blog/speak-app-pricing-per-month-2026)) |
| Premium Plus (monthly) | ~$30/mo (est.) | -- | Everything in Premium + unlimited custom lessons, "Made for You" personalized page ([Practice Me](https://practiceme.app/vs/speak)) |
| Premium Plus (annual) | -- | ~$12-15/mo (est.) | Same as monthly with annual discount |

Note: Prices vary by region; Korean market pricing differs from US pricing. Speak runs frequent regional promotions ([SpeakShark](https://speakshark.com/blog/speak-app-pricing-per-month-2026)).

## Enterprise/B2B Pricing

- **Speak for Business:** Custom pricing, available on request ([speak.com/b2b](https://www.speak.com/b2b))
- **Per-seat cost:** Not publicly disclosed
- **Minimum seats:** Unknown
- **Details:** Nascent B2B product launched in 2025; limited public information on enterprise pricing structure

## Government/Institutional Pricing

Not available.

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays $0/mo with limited daily access, or $8.25/mo (annual) for full access to all AI conversation features.
- **A serious learner** pays $12-15/mo (annual Premium Plus) for unlimited custom lessons and full conversation access.
- **An enterprise buyer** must request custom pricing; the B2B product is early-stage with limited features.

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | Speak | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| Free tier | Limited daily lessons | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry paid | ~$8.25/mo (annual) | $19/mo (Plus) |
| Mid-tier | ~$12-15/mo (Premium Plus annual) | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits) |
| Premium | N/A | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | Custom (early-stage) | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Included in subscription | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | Not available | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Speech AI quality | Best-in-class (OpenAI partnership) | TBD (Sep 2026 MVP) |
| Business content | None | Business English, skills, culture |
| CEFR certification | None | Included with assessment |

**Key Insight:** Speak is roughly half the price of Berlitz's entry tier (~$8 vs $19/mo) with arguably the best speech AI in market. However, it lacks human teachers, enterprise depth, certification, and business content. The gap is in outcomes and accountability, not in AI quality.
