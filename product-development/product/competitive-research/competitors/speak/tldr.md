## Product Summary

AI-powered speaking-first language learning app using OpenAI's Realtime API for natural voice conversations, structured lessons, and real-time pronunciation feedback ([Beginners in AI](https://beginnersinai.org/speak-explained/); [OpenAI Case Study](https://openai.com/index/speak-connor-zwick/)).

**Target Learner:** Intermediate learners who want to improve speaking fluency, particularly in English. Originally built for Korean English learners.

## Avatar/Visual AI Analysis

Speak does not have a visual avatar in the Praktika/Berlitz sense:

- **Implementation:** Character illustrations with voice personas (not animated 3D avatars) ([Practice Me](https://practiceme.app/vs/speak))
- **Interaction model:** Voice-first - the user speaks and the AI responds in real time. The UI shows text transcription alongside character images.
- **Comparison to Berlitz:** Speak's advantage is in speech technology quality (OpenAI partnership) rather than visual avatar engagement. Berlitz's planned avatar adds the visual dimension that Speak lacks while combining it with human teacher escalation.

## Strengths vs Berlitz

- Best-in-class speech AI (OpenAI Realtime API) with near-zero latency voice interaction ([Beginners in AI](https://beginnersinai.org/speak-explained/))
- $1B unicorn with deep OpenAI partnership and access to cutting-edge models ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/12/10/openai-backed-speak-raises-78m-at-1b-valuation-to-help-users-learn-languages-by-talking-out-loud/))
- Dominant market position in Korea with strong brand recognition
- Well-structured lesson curriculum with clear learning paths
- Strong mobile UX optimized for speaking practice
- ~50M learners demonstrate massive consumer traction ([Beginners in AI](https://beginnersinai.org/speak-explained/))
- Competitive price point (~$99/yr) ([SpeakShark](https://speakshark.com/blog/speak-app-pricing-per-month-2026))
- Forbes 2025 AI 50 list recognition ([Korea Times](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/business/tech-science/20250419/ai-based-english-learning-app-speak-makes-forbes-2025-ai-50-list))

## Weaknesses vs Berlitz

- No human teachers - pure AI with no escalation path
- No 3D avatar (just character illustrations)
- Nascent B2B offering with minimal enterprise features (no SSO, SCIM, LMS integration)
- No CEFR certification or formal assessment
- No business content or professional skills programs
- Consumer-focused pricing with no credit-based flexibility
- No offline mode

## Berlitz Positioning vs This Competitor

Speak has the best AI speech technology in the market, but it is a pure-AI consumer app with no human teacher network and minimal enterprise capabilities. Berlitz offers the hybrid model that Speak cannot - unlimited AI practice at Speak's quality level plus certified human instructors for coaching, assessment, and accountability. For enterprise buyers needing ROI measurement and compliance, Speak for Business is years behind Berlitz's depth.
