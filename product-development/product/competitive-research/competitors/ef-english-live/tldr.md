## Product Summary

Online English learning platform combining self-study courseware, live group classes, and 1:1 private lessons with certified teachers [EF English Live](https://englishlive.ef.com/en/). Part of the world's largest private education company [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/EF_Education_First). Features 3,000+ hours of interactive self-study content and the Efekta Hyperclass virtual classroom [EF English Live](https://englishlive.ef.com/en/method/hyperclass/).

**Target Learner:** Corporate employees needing business English, executives preparing for international roles, and ambitious individuals willing to invest in premium English instruction [EF Corporate Learning](https://corporatelearning.ef.com/en/solutions/corporate-english-training/).

## Avatar/Visual AI Analysis

EF English Live has no avatar:

- **Implementation:** Self-study courseware with 3,000+ hours of interactive content + live video classes with human teachers in Efekta Hyperclass [EF English Live](https://englishlive.ef.com/en/)
- **Interaction model:** Blended learning - learners complete self-study modules, then join group or 1:1 live classes for practice and assessment. Hyperclass adds VR-like scenarios for immersive practice [EF English Live Hyperclass](https://englishlive.ef.com/en/method/hyperclass/)
- **Comparison to Berlitz:** EF English Live is the most direct enterprise competitor to Berlitz. Both offer human teachers + self-study in a blended model. Berlitz differentiates with AI avatar practice (modern technology vs EF's traditional courseware) and credit-based flexible monetization.

## Berlitz Positioning vs This Competitor

EF English Live is Berlitz's most direct enterprise competitor - both offer blended human + self-study English learning for corporate clients. Berlitz differentiates on three fronts: (1) AI avatar practice that replaces EF's Hyperclass with dynamic, open-ended conversation, (2) credit-based monetization that gives enterprises flexible AI+human blending, and (3) multi-language, multi-skill programs (language + skills + culture) vs EF's English-only scope. For enterprise buyers evaluating EF vs Berlitz, the pitch is: "Next-generation AI practice, 140 years of teaching expertise, and programs beyond just English."
