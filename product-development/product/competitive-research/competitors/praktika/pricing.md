# Praktika - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Freemium with subscription. Free tier offers limited avatar access; paid subscription unlocks all avatars, unlimited conversations, and premium features. Single paid tier with 3-month and annual billing only, no month-to-month option ([Practice Me](https://practiceme.app/vs/praktika); [LanguaTalk](https://languatalk.com/blog/praktika-review/)).

## Consumer Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price (per month) | What's Included |
|------|--------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | Limited avatar access, restricted daily practice time ([Practice Me](https://practiceme.app/vs/praktika)) |
| Premium (3-month) | ~$13/mo | -- | All 5 avatars, unlimited conversations, pronunciation feedback, multimodal input ([Practice Me](https://practiceme.app/vs/praktika)) |
| Premium (annual) | -- | ~$8/mo ($96/yr) | Same as 3-month with ~38% savings ([Practice Me](https://practiceme.app/vs/praktika); [AI Chief](https://aichief.com/ai-education-tools/praktika-ai/)) |

Note: Pricing varies by country. No monthly billing option available ([LanguaTalk](https://languatalk.com/blog/praktika-review/)).

## Enterprise/B2B Pricing

Not available. Praktika has no enterprise or B2B offering ([TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/05/22/praktika-raises-35-5m-to-use-ai-avatars-to-make-learning-languages-feel-more-natural/)).

## Government/Institutional Pricing

Not available.

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays ~$8/mo (annual) for unlimited AI avatar conversations in up to 9 languages. No human teacher access at any price.
- **A serious learner** pays the same ~$8/mo. There is no premium tier with more features or human support. The ceiling is low.
- **An enterprise buyer** cannot purchase Praktika. No admin tools, no reporting, no team management.

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | Praktika | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Free tier | Limited avatar access | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry paid | ~$8/mo (annual) | $19/mo (Plus) |
| Mid-tier | N/A (single tier) | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits) |
| Premium | N/A | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | Not available | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Included in subscription | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | Not available | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Avatar AI | 5 animated 3D avatars | Planned AI avatar (Sep 2026 MVP) |
| Business content | None | Business English, skills, culture programs |
| CEFR certification | None | Included with assessment |

**Key Insight:** Praktika is ~4x cheaper than Berlitz's entry tier ($8 vs $19/mo) but offers only AI avatar practice with no human teachers, no structured outcomes, and no enterprise path. Berlitz justifies its premium through human coaching, certification, and enterprise depth.
