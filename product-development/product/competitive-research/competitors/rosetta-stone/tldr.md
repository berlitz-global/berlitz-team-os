# Rosetta Stone - Competitive Profile

## Company Profile

- **Founded:** 1992 (as Fairfield Language Technologies in Harrisonburg, VA; rebranded to Rosetta Stone in 2006) [About Rosetta Stone](https://www.rosettastone.com/about/company), [Grokipedia](https://grokipedia.com/page/Rosetta_Stone_Inc.)
- **HQ:** Arlington, VA (under IXL Learning in San Mateo, CA) [IXL Learning Acquires Rosetta Stone](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/ixl-learning-acquires-rosetta-stone-301249004.html)
- **Ownership:** Private subsidiary of IXL Learning (acquired 2021) [Cambium Completes Sale](https://www.cambiumlearning.com/about-us/news/5838/cambium-learningsup-sup-group-completes-its-sale-of-rosetta-stone-languages)
- **Total Funding:** N/A (was public; now private under IXL)
- **Est. Active Users:** Estimated 5-10M (across consumer, enterprise, and government); 4M app downloads in 2024 [Rosetta Stone Statistics](https://expandedramblings.com/index.php/rosetta-stone-statistics-facts/)
- **Est. Revenue/ARR:** Estimated $150-210M (not publicly disclosed post-acquisition; estimates vary by source) [CompWorth](https://compworth.com/company/rosetta-stone), [Growjo](https://growjo.com/company/Rosetta_Stone)
- **Employees:** ~755 (estimated; IXL Learning is larger) [Owler](https://www.owler.com/company/rosettastone), [ZoomInfo](https://www.zoominfo.com/c/rosetta-stone-ltd/64275274)

## Market & Geography

- **Segments:** B2B (enterprise, primary), B2C, B2G (military/government, significant) [Rosetta Stone Business](https://www.rosettastone.com/Business/)
- **Strongest Regions:** US (dominant), Europe, Japan, Korea [Rosetta Stone Statistics](https://electroiq.com/stats/rosetta-stone-statistics/)
- **Notable Contracts:** US Department of Defense (military language training), various federal agencies, Fortune 500 enterprises; solutions used by 22,000+ educational institutions, 12,000+ corporations, 9,000+ public sector organizations [Rosetta Stone Statistics](https://expandedramblings.com/index.php/rosetta-stone-statistics-facts/)
- **Languages Offered:** 25 languages (including Arabic, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and other strategic languages) [Rosetta Stone Languages](https://support.rosettastone.com/s/article/Languages-available-for-the-Rosetta-Stone-Mobile-Applications?language=en_US), [Complete List 2026](https://thebetterwebmovement.com/complete-list-of-languages-available-on-rosetta-stone-in-2026-including-new-additions/)

## Product Summary

Immersive language learning platform using proprietary TruAccent speech recognition and Dynamic Immersion methodology [Rosetta Stone TruAccent](https://www.rosettastone.com/features/truaccent-speech-recognition/), [Dynamic Immersion](https://www.rosettastone.com/features/dynamic-immersion/). Strong brand recognition with deep enterprise and government penetration [Rosetta Stone Business](https://www.rosettastone.com/Business/).

**Target Learner:** Enterprise employees, government/military personnel, and adult self-study learners who want structured immersive learning.

## Enterprise Features

Rosetta Stone has the deepest enterprise/government features among competitors [Rosetta Stone Enterprise](https://enterpriseblog.rosettastone.com/rosetta-stone-enterprise-how-it-works/):

- Admin portal: Yes - full enterprise admin console [Rosetta Stone Business](https://www.rosettastone.com/Business/)
- SSO/SAML: Yes (SAML 2.0, supporting IdP-initiated and SP-initiated authentication) [SAML SSO Guide](https://support.rosettastone.com/saml-sso/)
- SCIM: Available [Rosetta Stone Integration Options](https://support.rosettastone.com/s/article/Rosetta-Stone-Integration-Options?language=en_US)
- LMS/LTI integration: Yes - supports LMS integration via SP-initiated SSO and scheduled CSV/SFTP report transmissions [Integration Options](https://support.rosettastone.com/s/article/Rosetta-Stone-Integration-Options?language=en_US)
- Team reporting: Yes - detailed progress reports, compliance tracking [Rosetta Stone Enterprise](https://enterpriseblog.rosettastone.com/rosetta-stone-enterprise-how-it-works/)
- Content customization: Limited - can create learning paths [Rosetta Stone Enterprise](https://enterpriseblog.rosettastone.com/rosetta-stone-enterprise-how-it-works/)
- Dedicated CSM: Yes [Rosetta Stone Business](https://www.rosettastone.com/Business/)
- SLAs: Yes - enterprise SLAs available (Confidence: Medium)
- Compliance certs: SOC 2, FedRAMP (government) (Confidence: Medium - referenced widely but not confirmed on primary source)
- Data residency: Available (important for government) (Confidence: Medium)
- API access: Available for enterprise [Integration Options](https://support.rosettastone.com/s/article/Rosetta-Stone-Integration-Options?language=en_US)

## Avatar/Visual AI Analysis

Rosetta Stone has no avatar:

- **Implementation:** Traditional immersive learning with images, audio, and text. No animated characters or visual agents. [Rosetta Stone Review 2026](https://copycatcafe.com/blog/rosetta-stone-review)
- **Interaction model:** Self-paced lessons using Dynamic Immersion (no L1 translation), supplemented by 1:1 tutoring sessions. [Dynamic Immersion](https://www.rosettastone.com/features/dynamic-immersion/), [Enterprise Tutoring](https://enterpriseblog.rosettastone.com/new-tutoring-topics/)
- **Comparison to Berlitz:** Rosetta Stone represents the previous generation of language learning technology. Berlitz's AI avatar approach is a generational leap forward while maintaining the structured, outcome-driven approach that enterprise buyers expect.

## Berlitz Positioning vs This Competitor

Rosetta Stone is a displacement opportunity. Enterprise and government buyers currently using Rosetta Stone get outdated technology, no AI conversation practice, and limited human coaching. Berlitz offers a next-generation alternative - AI avatar practice, certified human instructors, CEFR outcomes, and modern enterprise features - while matching Rosetta Stone's compliance and integration depth. The pitch: "Everything Rosetta Stone promised, now delivered with AI and human coaching at modern quality."

## Methodology

Research conducted June 2026 using 10+ web searches with varied queries covering: company history and acquisition, pricing tiers, product features (TruAccent, Fluency Builder, Dynamic Immersion), enterprise/government capabilities, and recent product updates. Primary sources (rosettastone.com, enterprise blog, support documentation) were prioritized. Revenue and employee figures are third-party estimates as Rosetta Stone is no longer publicly traded. Compliance certifications (SOC 2, FedRAMP) are widely referenced in secondary sources but could not be confirmed on a primary Rosetta Stone compliance page during this research cycle.
