# Rosetta Stone - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Subscription or lifetime purchase for consumers. Custom enterprise licensing. Government contracting via GSA schedule. [Rosetta Stone Pricing](https://testprepinsight.com/resources/how-much-does-rosetta-stone-cost/), [LinguaSteps Pricing Overview](https://linguasteps.com/reviews/rosetta-stone-pricing-a-transparent-overview)

## Enterprise/B2B Pricing

- **Enterprise licensing:** Custom pricing, starts at ~$10/user/month or less [Software Finder](https://softwarefinder.com/lms/rosetta-stone) (Confidence: Medium)
- **Minimum seats:** Varies (typically 25+) (Confidence: Medium)
- **Contract terms:** Annual or multi-year [Rosetta Stone Enterprise](https://enterpriseblog.rosettastone.com/rosetta-stone-enterprise-how-it-works/)
- **Features included:** Admin console, SSO/SAML, LMS integration, progress reports, compliance tracking, dedicated CSM [Rosetta Stone Business](https://www.rosettastone.com/Business/), [SAML SSO](https://support.rosettastone.com/saml-sso/)
- **Government pricing:** Available via GSA schedule; often bundled with multi-year contracts (Confidence: Medium)

## Government/Institutional Pricing

- **Military/DoD:** Rosetta Stone provides language training to military personnel and eligible family members. Pricing is via government contracting (GSA schedule). [Rosetta Stone Statistics](https://expandedramblings.com/index.php/rosetta-stone-statistics-facts/) (Confidence: Medium - DoD relationship confirmed, specific pricing terms not public)
- **Federal agencies:** FedRAMP-compliant deployment available (Confidence: Medium - widely referenced but not confirmed on primary compliance page)
- **Academic:** Educational institution pricing available; often site-licensed. Used by 22,000+ educational institutions. [Rosetta Stone Statistics](https://expandedramblings.com/index.php/rosetta-stone-statistics-facts/)

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays ~$10.50/mo (annual subscription) for self-paced immersive learning in 1 language, or ~$149-199 lifetime for all 25 languages during promotions. [TalkReal](https://talkreal.org/en/blog/rosetta-stone-costs/), [PopSci](https://www.popsci.com/gear/rosetta-stone-new-years-deal-2026/)
- **A serious learner** pays ~$10.50/mo + $15-25/session for optional 1:1 tutoring. At 4 sessions/month, that is ~$70-110/month. [TestPrepInsight](https://testprepinsight.com/resources/how-much-does-rosetta-stone-cost/)
- **An enterprise buyer** pays ~$10/user/month or less for team licensing with full admin and compliance features. [Software Finder](https://softwarefinder.com/lms/rosetta-stone) (Confidence: Medium)
- **A government buyer** pays via GSA schedule - often $5-10/user/month at scale. (Confidence: Low - specific government per-user rates not publicly confirmed)

**Key Insight:** Rosetta Stone's enterprise pricing (~$10/user/mo) is competitive, and government buyers are locked into GSA schedules. However, the product is technologically outdated. Berlitz can win displacement deals by offering modern AI + human hybrid delivery at comparable enterprise pricing, especially when contracts come up for renewal. The lifetime consumer product ($149-199 on promo) is a one-time purchase that generates no ongoing revenue - a model Berlitz should not replicate.

## Methodology

Pricing research conducted June 2026 via 10+ web searches covering consumer subscription tiers, lifetime deals, enterprise licensing, and government contracting. Consumer prices were cross-referenced across 3+ independent review/deal sites. Enterprise and government pricing is estimated, as Rosetta Stone does not publish these rates publicly. Promotional pricing (lifetime deals at $149-199) was confirmed via deal coverage from Variety, PopSci, and 9to5Toys. The comparison table's Berlitz column reflects internal target pricing and is not sourced externally.
