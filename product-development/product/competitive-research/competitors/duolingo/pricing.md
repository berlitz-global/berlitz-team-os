# Duolingo - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Freemium with tiered subscriptions. The free tier is the core product (ad-supported); paid tiers remove ads and add AI features. Duolingo English Test is a separate paid product ($65/test). ([Duolingo](https://www.duolingo.com/); [DealNews](https://www.dealnews.com/features/duolingo/cost/); [Duolingo English Test](https://englishtest.duolingo.com/institutions))

## Consumer Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price (per month) | What's Included |
|------|--------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | Full course access, ads, limited hearts/lives, Explain My Answer AI |
| Super Duolingo (monthly) | $12.99/mo | - | No ads, unlimited hearts, progress tracking, mistake review |
| Super Duolingo (annual) | - | ~$8/mo ($95.99/yr) | Same as monthly with annual discount |
| Duolingo Max (monthly) | $29.99/mo | - | Everything in Super + Video Call, role-play, advanced AI features |
| Duolingo Max (annual) | - | $14/mo ($168/yr) | Same as monthly with annual discount |
| Super Family Plan (annual) | - | ~$3.33/person/mo ($119.99/yr for 6) | Super features for up to 6 family members |

([DealNews](https://www.dealnews.com/features/duolingo/cost/); [LinguaSteps](https://linguasteps.com/reviews/duolingo-pricing-a-transparent-overview); [Spliiit](https://www.spliiit.com/en/blog/duolingo-prix-famille); [FamilyPro](https://familypro.io/en/how-much-is-super-duolingo/))

Note: Prices vary by region. Not all tiers are available in all markets. ([LinguaSteps](https://linguasteps.com/reviews/duolingo-pricing-a-transparent-overview))

## Enterprise/B2B Pricing

- **Duolingo for Business:** Custom quote required; estimates range from ~$50-68/learner/year depending on volume, billed annually ([PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/duolingo-pricing/); [Duolingo for Business](https://www.duolingo.com/business))
- **Minimum seats:** Varies by plan ([Duolingo for Business](https://www.duolingo.com/business))
- **Details:** Basic admin dashboard, team progress reports, no custom content ([G2](https://www.g2.com/products/duolingo-for-business/reviews))

## Government/Institutional Pricing

- **Duolingo English Test:** $65 per test attempt; accepted by 6,000+ institutions in 110+ countries ([Duolingo English Test](https://englishtest.duolingo.com/institutions); [LumeTest](https://lumetest.com/blog/duolingo-english-test/duolingo-english-test-det-acceptance-where-is-it-accepted))
- **Institutional partnerships:** Some universities offer Duolingo as supplementary tool ([Capterra](https://www.capterra.com/p/210401/Duolingo-for-Schools/))

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays $0 and gets a complete (ad-supported) language course in 40+ languages. This is the benchmark for free language learning. ([Duolingo](https://www.duolingo.com/); [DealNews](https://www.dealnews.com/features/duolingo/cost/))
- **A serious learner** pays $14/mo (Max annual) for Video Call AI conversations, role-play, and all premium features. ([LinguaSteps](https://linguasteps.com/reviews/duolingo-pricing-a-transparent-overview))
- **An enterprise buyer** pays ~$50-68/learner/year for basic team features with no custom content or human teachers. ([PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/duolingo-pricing/))

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | Duolingo | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Free tier | Full course (ad-supported) | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry paid | ~$8/mo (Super annual) | $19/mo (Plus) |
| Mid-tier | $14/mo (Max annual) | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits) |
| Premium | $29.99/mo (Max monthly) | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | ~$50-68/learner/year | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Free (core product) | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | Not available | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Gamification | Best-in-class | TBD (Sep 2026 MVP) |
| Languages | 40+ (280+ courses) | Multiple (TBD count) |
| CEFR certification | Separate product ($65/test) | Included with learning program |
| Business content | None | Business English, skills, culture |
| Family plan | $119.99/yr for 6 | Not yet available |

([DealNews](https://www.dealnews.com/features/duolingo/cost/); [LinguaSteps](https://linguasteps.com/reviews/duolingo-pricing-a-transparent-overview); [Duolingo IR - Course Launch](https://investors.duolingo.com/news-releases/news-release-details/duolingo-launches-148-new-language-courses); [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/duolingo-pricing/))

**Key Insight:** Duolingo sets the floor for price and the ceiling for engagement. Berlitz cannot compete on price or scale with Duolingo's free tier. Instead, Berlitz competes on outcomes: human coaching, certification, enterprise depth, and business content that Duolingo does not offer at any price. The positioning is complementary for casual learners (Duolingo) vs outcome-driven learners (Berlitz).

## Methodology

Pricing data collected June 2026 from 4+ independent pricing comparison sites (DealNews, LinguaSteps, Spliiit, FamilyPro) and cross-referenced for consistency. Consumer pricing reflects US prices; regional variation exists. Enterprise pricing is estimated from PricingNow (Medium confidence) as Duolingo does not publicly disclose B2B rates. Duolingo English Test pricing confirmed via the official DET site. Language/course counts sourced from Duolingo investor relations press releases.
