## Product Summary

Free-to-play gamified language learning platform with bite-sized lessons, streaks, leaderboards, and AI-powered features including video calls and role-play. The global benchmark for consumer language learning. ([Duolingo Blog](https://blog.duolingo.com/duolingo-max/); [Duolingo IR](https://investors.duolingo.com/company-strategy-overview-0))

**Target Learner:** Mass market, from casual hobbyists to serious learners. Strongest with younger demographics (Gen Z, millennials) who value gamification and free access. ([Duolingo 2025 Language Report](https://blog.duolingo.com/2025-duolingo-language-report/))

## Enterprise Features

Duolingo for Business exists but is thin for enterprise needs ([Duolingo for Business](https://www.duolingo.com/business); [G2](https://www.g2.com/products/duolingo-for-business/reviews)):

- Admin portal: Yes, basic team management dashboard
- SSO/SAML: Limited (available for large accounts)
- SCIM: No
- LMS/LTI integration: Limited
- Team reporting: Basic progress and engagement reports
- Content customization: No
- Dedicated CSM: For large accounts
- SLAs: No public SLA
- Compliance certs: No
- Data residency: No
- API access: No public API

## Avatar/Visual AI Analysis

Duolingo uses animated characters but not realistic avatars ([Duolingo Blog](https://blog.duolingo.com/duolingo-max/)):

- **Implementation:** Duo the owl mascot and animated teacher characters appear in the Video Call feature (Max tier). Characters are cartoon-style with voice synthesis.
- **Interaction model:** Video Call simulates a face-to-face conversation with an animated character. The character speaks, the user responds by voice, and the AI continues the conversation. ([Duolingo IR - Duocon 2025](https://investors.duolingo.com/news-releases/news-release-details/duolingo-unveils-major-product-updates-turn-learning-real-world))
- **Comparison to Berlitz:** Duolingo's animated characters are playful/cartoon-style, targeting casual engagement. Berlitz's planned avatar aims for professional/realistic interaction suited to business learners with CEFR-aligned outcomes.

- 40+ languages across 280+ courses, broadest offering in market ([Duolingo IR - Course Launch](https://investors.duolingo.com/news-releases/news-release-details/duolingo-launches-148-new-language-courses))

- Public company with strong financials (~$1.01B FY2025 revenue, profitable) ([Duolingo Q4 FY2025 8-K](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1562088/000162828026012246/q4fy25duolingo12-31x25shar.htm))
- Video Call feature adds conversational practice at scale ([Duolingo IR - Duocon 2025](https://investors.duolingo.com/news-releases/news-release-details/duolingo-unveils-major-product-updates-turn-learning-real-world))

## Weaknesses vs Berlitz

- No human teachers at any tier ([Duolingo for Business](https://www.duolingo.com/business))
- Shallow learning outcomes, good for beginners but limited for intermediate/advanced (courses now reaching B2 in 9 languages as of 2026) ([Duolingo IR - Duocon 2025](https://investors.duolingo.com/news-releases/news-release-details/duolingo-unveils-major-product-updates-turn-learning-real-world))
- No business content or professional skills training ([G2](https://www.g2.com/products/duolingo-for-business/reviews))
- Gamification can prioritize engagement over actual learning
- Enterprise features are minimal compared to dedicated B2B platforms ([G2](https://www.g2.com/products/duolingo-for-business/reviews))
- No CEFR certification (Duolingo English Test is separate from the learning app) ([Duolingo English Test](https://englishtest.duolingo.com/institutions))
- Cartoon aesthetic doesn't suit professional/corporate environments
- No credit-based system for flexible human+AI blending
- No cultural or skills programs beyond language

## Berlitz Positioning vs This Competitor

Duolingo owns the free/casual language learning market with unmatched scale and gamification, but stops at surface-level fluency. Berlitz serves the next level: learners and enterprises that need real communication outcomes, human coaching, CEFR certification, and business skills. Berlitz's free tier offers unlimited AI practice (comparable to Duolingo) plus human teacher demos, while paid tiers unlock what Duolingo cannot: certified instructors, enterprise ROI dashboards, and business communication programs.
