# Lingoda - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Subscription with credit-based class booking [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/). Monthly plans include a set number of group or private classes. No long-term contracts required - pay-as-you-go monthly [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/). Sprint model offers intensive 2-month programs with cashback incentives for new customers [Lingoda Sprint](https://www.lingoda.com/en/sprint/). Free 7-day trial available [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/).

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays ~$89/mo for 4 group classes (1 per week), or ~$22/class. [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/)
- **A serious learner** pays ~$139/mo for 12 group classes (3 per week), or ~$12/class. Adding private classes increases cost to $200+/mo. [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/)
- **A Sprint participant** pays $225-445 upfront for 2 months of intensive classes (15/month). If they complete the challenge, they receive 50% cashback. Super Sprint participants attend 30 classes/month and can receive up to 100% cashback. Only available to new customers. [Lingoda Sprint Prices](https://www.lingoda.com/en/sprint/prices/), [Lingoda Sprint Rules](https://lingoda-students.elevio.help/en/articles/261-what-are-the-sprint-promotion-rules)
- **An enterprise buyer** pays custom pricing per seat for team training with progress reports and ROI tracking. [Lingoda Corporate](https://www.lingoda.com/en/p/corporate-learning-and-development/)

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | Lingoda | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Free tier | No free tier (7-day trial only) [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/) | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry paid (4 classes/mo) | ~$89/mo [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/) | $19/mo (Plus, 2 AI tutor sessions) |
| Mid-tier (8 classes/mo) | ~$109/mo [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/) | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits incl. human) |
| Premium (12 classes/mo) | ~$139/mo [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/) | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | Custom pricing/seat [Lingoda Corporate](https://www.lingoda.com/en/p/corporate-learning-and-development/) | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Not available | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | $8-48/class [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/) | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Class format | Group (3-5) + optional 1:1 [Lingoda Flex](https://www.lingoda.com/en/flex/) | 1:1 + group |
| Class duration | 60 minutes [Lingoda Flex](https://www.lingoda.com/en/flex/) | Varies |
| CEFR certification | Yes [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/) | Yes |
| Sprint/intensive option | Yes (50-100% cashback, new customers only) [Lingoda Sprint](https://www.lingoda.com/en/sprint/) | Not yet |
| Languages | 5 + Business English [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/) | Multiple (TBD count) |

**Key Insight:** Lingoda is 2-3x more expensive than Berlitz for comparable human teaching access ($89-139/mo vs $49-99/mo) and includes zero AI practice between classes. Berlitz's credit system delivers more flexible human + AI practice at a lower total cost. The Sprint model is clever for acquisition but has complex cashback rules (new customers only, strict attendance requirements, 40-45 day payout timeline) [Lingoda Sprint Rules](https://lingoda-students.elevio.help/en/articles/261-what-are-the-sprint-promotion-rules). Berlitz's free AI tier is a simpler and more sustainable acquisition hook.
