- **Strongest Regions:** Germany (home market), Europe, growing in US and Latin America [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2021/04/15/lingoda-an-on-demand-online-language-school-with-live-instructors-and-zoom-classrooms-raises-67m/)

## Product Summary

Online language school offering live 60-minute group classes (3-5 students max) and 1:1 private lessons with certified native-speaking teachers [Lingoda Flex](https://www.lingoda.com/en/flex/). Known for the Sprint model (intensive 2-month programs with cashback incentive) [Lingoda Sprint](https://www.lingoda.com/en/sprint/). Delivers approximately 800,000-1,000,000 live classes annually [Lingoda Press](https://www.lingoda.com/en/press/), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lingoda).

**Target Learner:** Motivated adults who want structured live classes with real teachers in small groups or 1:1. Strong appeal to professionals preparing for relocation, certification, or career transitions [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/).

## Avatar/Visual AI Analysis

Lingoda has no avatar or AI:

- **Implementation:** All teaching is done by live human teachers via Zoom video platform. No AI component. [Lingoda Flex](https://www.lingoda.com/en/flex/), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lingoda)
- **Interaction model:** Learners join scheduled 60-minute live classes with a teacher and 2-4 other students, or book private 1:1 sessions. [Lingoda Flex](https://www.lingoda.com/en/flex/)
- **Comparison to Berlitz:** Lingoda is the closest model to traditional Berlitz (live human teaching) but without any AI layer. Berlitz adds AI avatar practice for unlimited daily engagement between human sessions.

## Strengths vs Berlitz

- Live human teaching at scale (800K-1M+ annual lessons, 2,400-2,500 certified teachers) [Lingoda Press](https://www.lingoda.com/en/press/), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lingoda)
- CEFR certificates upon level completion [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/)
- Small group sizes (3-5 students max) ensure quality interaction [Lingoda Flex](https://www.lingoda.com/en/flex/)
- Sprint model drives engagement (intensive 2-month programs with 50% cashback for Sprint, 100% for Super Sprint) [Lingoda Sprint](https://www.lingoda.com/en/sprint/)
- Strong funding ($68-90M raised, Series C from Summit Partners) [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2021/04/15/lingoda-an-on-demand-online-language-school-with-live-instructors-and-zoom-classrooms-raises-67m/)
- Corporate language training for B2B with progress reports and ROI tracking [Lingoda Corporate](https://www.lingoda.com/en/p/corporate-learning-and-development/)
- EU-based with GDPR compliance [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/)
- 60-minute classes (longer than many competitors) [Lingoda Flex](https://www.lingoda.com/en/flex/)
- Affordable per-class pricing (~$8-23/class in group format depending on plan) [Lingoda Pricing](https://www.lingoda.com/en/pricing/)

## Weaknesses vs Berlitz

- Zero AI capability - no conversation AI, no avatar, no speech technology [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/)
- Only 5 languages plus Business English (English, German, French, Spanish, Italian) [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/)
- Italian only available at beginner levels (A1-A2) [Lingoda Italian](https://www.lingoda.com/en/italian/)
- No self-study content between live classes [FluentU Lingoda Review](https://www.fluentu.com/blog/reviews/lingoda/)
- No offline mode
- Sprint model has complex rules and mixed reviews (cashback conditions, new customers only for rewards) [Lingoda Sprint Rules](https://lingoda-students.elevio.help/en/articles/261-what-are-the-sprint-promotion-rules)
- No business skills or cultural programs beyond Business English [Lingoda Homepage](https://www.lingoda.com/en/)
- Enterprise features are basic compared to Berlitz's depth [Preply vs Lingoda Comparison](https://preply.com/en/blog/b2b-preply-business-lingoda-review/)
- Teacher continuity is limited - different teacher each class (group model) [FluentU Lingoda Review](https://www.fluentu.com/blog/reviews/lingoda/)
- Trustpilot ratings are polarized (4.3-4.5/5 overall but also vocal dissatisfied users, 1.9/5 on some consumer review sites) [Trustpilot](https://www.trustpilot.com/review/lingoda.com), [MyEngineeringBuddy](https://www.myengineeringbuddy.com/blog/lingoda-reviews-alternatives-pricing-offerings/)

## Berlitz Positioning vs This Competitor

Lingoda validates the market for live human teaching at scale but offers zero AI between classes. Berlitz combines Lingoda's human teaching quality with unlimited AI practice, allowing learners to practice daily for free and use human sessions for coaching and assessment. For enterprise buyers, Berlitz adds ROI dashboards, business skills programs, and deeper admin controls. The pitch: "All the human teaching quality of Lingoda, plus unlimited AI practice, at a comparable price."

## Methodology

Research conducted June 2026 using 8 web searches across company financials, pricing, product features, B2B offerings, funding history, Sprint program rules, user reviews, and language offerings. Primary sources (lingoda.com pages) were prioritized for product and pricing claims. Company financials were cross-referenced across Tracxn, Owler, Crunchbase, CB Insights, and PitchBook, with conflicts noted. Revenue figures could not be independently verified and are flagged as Low confidence. Teacher count and class volume updated from Lingoda press materials and Wikipedia. Sprint cashback rules verified against official Lingoda help center documentation and rules PDFs.
