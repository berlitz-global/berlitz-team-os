# ELSA - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Freemium with subscription. Free tier with limited features; premium subscription unlocks full pronunciation AI and personalized learning paths. Separate B2B pricing per user ([SaaSworthy](https://www.saasworthy.com/product/elsa-speak/pricing); [Capterra](https://www.capterra.com/p/240698/ELSA-Speak/pricing/)).

## Government/Institutional Pricing

ELSA offers school plans with per-student pricing, typically aligned with business plan rates. Custom quotes for educational institutions ([elsaspeak.com/en/english-for-schools/plans](https://elsaspeak.com/en/english-for-schools/plans)).

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays $0 with limited access, or ~$6-8/mo (annual) for full pronunciation coaching in English only.
- **A serious learner** pays ~$16.59/mo for full access with ELSA AI chatbot and role-play scenarios.
- **An enterprise buyer** pays ~$18.20/user/month for the Team Plan with admin dashboard, or negotiates custom pricing for larger deployments.

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | ELSA | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|------|---------|
| Free tier | Limited pronunciation exercises | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry paid | ~$6-8/mo (annual) | $19/mo (Plus) |
| Mid-tier | $16.59/mo (monthly) | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits) |
| Premium | $100-150 lifetime | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | $18.20/user/month | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Included in subscription | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | Not available | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Pronunciation AI | Best-in-class phoneme scoring | Planned (Sep 2026 MVP) |
| Languages | English only | Multiple languages |
| Business content | Pronunciation in business contexts | Full business skills programs |

**Key Insight:** ELSA is significantly cheaper than Berlitz for pure pronunciation training but covers only English and only pronunciation. The B2B pricing ($18.20/user/mo) is comparable to Berlitz's per-credit cost, but Berlitz delivers broader outcomes (fluency, business skills, certification, human coaching).
