## Product Summary

Freemium language learning app with CEFR-aligned curriculum, community correction features, and official certificates [Source: [Busuu Certification Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/languages/certification)]. Part of Chegg's education platform since 2022 [Source: [Chegg Acquisition Announcement](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20211129005427/en/Chegg-to-Enter-Rapidly-Expanding-Digital-Language-Learning-Market-with-Acquisition-of-Busuu)]. Chegg is now strategically repositioning around Busuu as part of its "Skilling" B2B pivot [Source: [Investing.com Q4 2025 Coverage](https://www.investing.com/news/company-news/chegg-q4-2025-slides-show-revenue-beat-strategic-shift-to-workforce-skilling-93CH-4495282)].

**Target Learner:** Intermediate learners who value structured CEFR progression and certification. Community-oriented learners who enjoy peer correction [Source: [Busuu Course Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/it-works/courses)].

## Avatar/Visual AI Analysis

Busuu has no avatar:

- **Implementation:** Traditional app-based learning with community features (native speakers correct your exercises) [Source: [Busuu Course Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/it-works/courses)]
- **Interaction model:** Self-paced courses + community corrections + optional business courses [Source: [Preply vs Busuu Comparison](https://preply.com/en/blog/b2b-preply-business-vs-busuu-for-business/)]
- **Comparison to Berlitz:** Busuu's unique feature is community peer correction, which is fundamentally different from both AI avatar interaction and professional human tutoring. Berlitz offers professional AI + human coaching rather than peer-based correction.

- 120-140M registered users (large funnel, even if conversion is low) [Source: [Busuu Press Centre](https://www.busuu.com/en/press); [Business of Apps](https://www.businessofapps.com/data/busuu-statistics/)]

- Affordable pricing (~$6/mo annual) [Source: [Busuu Premium Plans Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/premium-plans)]
- 14 languages with structured courses [Source: [Busuu Support](https://help.busuu.com/hc/en-us/articles/16519527128849-How-many-languages-can-I-learn)]
- Part of Chegg - access to broader education platform and resources [Source: [Chegg Acquisition Announcement](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20211129005427/en/Chegg-to-Enter-Rapidly-Expanding-Digital-Language-Learning-Market-with-Acquisition-of-Busuu)]
- Business courses with certification in English, French, Dutch [Source: [Busuu for Business](https://business.busuu.com/resources/language-training-programs-business)] (Medium confidence)
- EU-based with GDPR compliance [Source: [Busuu - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Busuu)] (Medium confidence)

## Weaknesses vs Berlitz

- Weak AI capabilities - no conversational AI, no avatar, no LLM [Source: [Preply vs Busuu Comparison](https://preply.com/en/blog/b2b-preply-business-vs-busuu-for-business/); [TestPrepInsight Review](https://testprepinsight.com/reviews/busuu-review/)]
- No human teachers (community corrections are not professional tutoring) [Source: [Preply vs Busuu Comparison](https://preply.com/en/blog/b2b-preply-business-vs-busuu-for-business/)]
- McGraw-Hill certification partnership discontinued (now Busuu-branded only) [Source: [Busuu McGraw-Hill Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/how-to/mcgraw-hill-certificates); [Busuu Support - Certificates](https://help.busuu.com/hc/en-us/articles/16559434818321-What-are-Certificates-and-how-can-I-get-them)]
- Part of Chegg, which has struggled financially (may limit investment) [Source: [Chegg Q4 2025 Earnings](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260209620934/en/Chegg-Reports-2025-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-Financial-Results) - EPS of -$0.01]
- B2B offering is basic compared to Berlitz's enterprise depth [Source: [Preply vs Busuu Comparison](https://preply.com/en/blog/b2b-preply-business-vs-busuu-for-business/)]
- No 1:1 or group live classes [Source: [Lingoda vs Busuu Comparison](https://www.lingoda.com/blog/en/lingoda-vs-busuu/)]
- No business skills or cultural programs [Source: [Preply vs Busuu Comparison](https://preply.com/en/blog/b2b-preply-business-vs-busuu-for-business/)] (Medium confidence)
- Community corrections are inconsistent in quality [Source: [Multilingualmastery Review](https://multilingualmastery.com/busuu-review/)] (Medium confidence)
- Revenue is modest (Skilling segment ~$18M/quarter) relative to user base (low conversion) [Source: [Investing.com Q4 2025 Coverage](https://www.investing.com/news/company-news/chegg-q4-2025-slides-show-revenue-beat-strategic-shift-to-workforce-skilling-93CH-4495282)]

## Berlitz Positioning vs This Competitor

Busuu's CEFR certification is its strongest feature, but the certificates are now Busuu-branded (no longer McGraw-Hill joint) [Source: [Busuu Support - Certificates](https://help.busuu.com/hc/en-us/articles/16559434818321-What-are-Certificates-and-how-can-I-get-them)]. Berlitz offers CEFR-aligned assessment with the added weight of the Berlitz brand - 140+ years of language education credibility - plus human coaching, AI avatar practice, and enterprise ROI dashboards. For enterprise buyers, Berlitz's outcome measurement and instructor quality far exceed Busuu's community-based model.

## Methodology

Research conducted June 2026 using 8 web searches across varied queries covering: Chegg/Busuu financials and ownership, pricing tiers, product features, B2B offering, CEFR certification, user statistics, and language coverage. Primary sources (Chegg SEC filings, Busuu official pages, Chegg investor relations) were prioritized. Claims were cross-referenced across multiple sources where possible. Revenue figures come from Chegg's public earnings releases and may reflect the broader "Skilling" segment rather than Busuu alone. User counts vary across sources due to different measurement dates and methodologies.
