# Busuu - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Freemium with two paid tiers [Source: [Busuu Premium Plans Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/premium-plans)]. Free tier provides access to basic lessons; Premium and Premium Plus unlock full features and certificates [Source: [Busuu Support - What is Premium?](https://help.busuu.com/hc/en-us/articles/16269241908497-What-is-Premium)]. (High confidence)

## Consumer Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price (per month) | What's Included |
|------|--------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | $0 | Basic lessons, limited community features [Source: [Busuu Premium Plans Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/premium-plans)] |
| Premium (monthly) | ~$13.99/mo | - | Full courses in 14 languages, grammar review, offline mode [Source: [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/busuu-pricing/); [SBI Growth](https://sbigrowth.com/insights/busuu-duolingo-pricing)] |
| Premium (annual) | - | ~$5.83/mo (~$70/yr) | Same as monthly with annual discount [Source: [LinguaSteps](https://linguasteps.com/resources/busuu-pricing-a-transparent-overview); [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/busuu-pricing/)] |
| Premium Plus (monthly) | ~$15.99/mo | - | Everything in Premium + CEFR certificates, advanced grammar [Source: [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/busuu-pricing/)] |
| Premium Plus (annual) | - | ~$6.66/mo (~$80/yr) | Same with annual discount [Source: [LinguaSteps](https://linguasteps.com/resources/busuu-pricing-a-transparent-overview); [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/busuu-pricing/)] |

Note: Some 2026 sources suggest Busuu may be consolidating Premium and Premium Plus into a single tier [Source: [Univext Review 2026](https://univext.com/en/blog/340/busuu-review-2026)]. Pricing may vary by platform, region, and active promotions [Source: [LinguaSteps](https://linguasteps.com/resources/busuu-pricing-a-transparent-overview)]. (Medium confidence on tier consolidation)

## Enterprise/B2B Pricing

- **Busuu for Organisations:** Custom pricing per seat [Source: [SoftwareAdvice](https://www.softwareadvice.com/lms/busuu-profile/); [Capterra](https://www.capterra.com/p/210762/Busuu/pricing/)] (High confidence)
- **Estimated per-seat cost:** $8-20/user/month depending on volume [Source: [TrustRadius](https://www.trustradius.com/products/busuu-for-business/pricing)] (Medium confidence)
- **Minimum seats:** Typically 10+ (Medium confidence)
- **Features included:** Admin dashboard (Management Platform), progress tracking, course assignment, business courses with certification [Source: [Busuu for Business](https://business.busuu.com/product)]
- **Volume discounts:** Available [Source: [SoftwareAdvice](https://www.softwareadvice.com/lms/busuu-profile/)] (Medium confidence)

## Government/Institutional Pricing

Busuu offers educational institution pricing. Details available on request [Source: [Busuu for Business](https://business.busuu.com/product)]. (Low confidence - no public pricing found)

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays $0 for basic access (ad-supported), or $5.83/mo (annual Premium) for full course access [Source: [Busuu Premium Plans Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/premium-plans)].
- **A serious learner** pays $6.66/mo (annual Premium Plus) for CEFR certificates and advanced features [Source: [Busuu Premium Plans Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/premium-plans)].
- **An enterprise buyer** pays ~$8-20/user/month for organizational features with admin dashboard [Source: [TrustRadius](https://www.trustradius.com/products/busuu-for-business/pricing)] (Medium confidence).

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | Busuu | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|-------|---------|
| Free tier | Basic lessons [Source: [Busuu Premium Plans Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/premium-plans)] | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry paid | $5.83/mo (Premium annual) [Source: [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/busuu-pricing/)] | $19/mo (Plus) |
| Mid-tier | $6.66/mo (Premium Plus annual) [Source: [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/busuu-pricing/)] | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits) |
| Premium | $15.99/mo (monthly) [Source: [PricingNow](https://pricingnow.com/question/busuu-pricing/)] | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | $8-20/user/month [Source: [TrustRadius](https://www.trustradius.com/products/busuu-for-business/pricing)] | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Included (limited AI) [Source: [TestPrepInsight Review](https://testprepinsight.com/reviews/busuu-review/)] | Free on all tiers (unlimited AI) |
| Human teacher cost | Not available (community only) [Source: [Lingoda vs Busuu](https://www.lingoda.com/blog/en/lingoda-vs-busuu/)] | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| CEFR certification | Yes (Busuu-branded) [Source: [Busuu Support - Certificates](https://help.busuu.com/hc/en-us/articles/16559434818321-What-are-Certificates-and-how-can-I-get-them)] | Yes (Berlitz-branded, human-assessed) |
| Community features | Native speaker corrections [Source: [Busuu Course Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/it-works/courses)] | Not a feature |
| Business content | Business courses (limited) [Source: [Busuu for Business](https://business.busuu.com/resources/language-training-programs-business)] | Full business programs |

**Key Insight:** Busuu is roughly 3x cheaper than Berlitz's entry tier ($5.83 vs $19/mo) and offers CEFR certificates [Source: [Busuu Premium Plans Page](https://www.busuu.com/en/premium-plans)]. However, Busuu has no human teachers, no AI conversation practice, and relies on community corrections [Source: [Preply vs Busuu Comparison](https://preply.com/en/blog/b2b-preply-business-vs-busuu-for-business/)]. Berlitz justifies its premium with professional human coaching, AI avatar practice, and enterprise-grade outcomes. For buyers who need certificates without depth, Busuu is sufficient; for those who need actual fluency and business outcomes, Berlitz delivers more.

## Methodology

Research conducted June 2026 using 8 web searches with varied queries covering Busuu pricing tiers, B2B enterprise costs, and feature comparisons. Consumer pricing was cross-referenced across 3+ independent sources (PricingNow, LinguaSteps, SBI Growth, Busuu official site). Enterprise pricing is estimated from review aggregator sites since Busuu does not publish B2B pricing publicly. Monthly prices may fluctuate by region, platform (iOS vs web), and promotional offers. All prices are in USD.
