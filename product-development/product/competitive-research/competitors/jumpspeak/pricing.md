# Jumpspeak - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Freemium with tiered subscription. Free trial (7 days); Premium subscription unlocks lessons; Premium AI add-on removes AI Tutor daily limits. Lifetime option available. Live tutoring via Rype requires a separate subscription ([Jumpspeak Pricing](https://www.jumpspeak.com/pricing); [Top10](https://www.top10.com/language-learning/reviews/jumpspeak)).

## Consumer Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price (per month) | What's Included |
|------|--------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| Free trial | $0 (7 days) | -- | Limited access to lessons ([Top10](https://www.top10.com/language-learning/reviews/jumpspeak)) |
| Premium (quarterly) | ~$23/mo ($69/quarter) | -- | Full lesson library (1,000+ lessons), limited AI Tutor chats, speech recognition ([Jumpspeak Pricing](https://www.jumpspeak.com/pricing)) |
| Premium (annual) | -- | ~$8.25/mo ($99/yr) | Same as quarterly with annual discount ([Jumpspeak Pricing](https://www.jumpspeak.com/pricing); [Top10](https://www.top10.com/language-learning/reviews/jumpspeak)) |
| Lifetime | $249 one-time | -- | Permanent access to all features ([Jumpspeak Pricing](https://www.jumpspeak.com/pricing)) |
| Live Tutoring (via Rype) | ~$79-199/mo (separate) | -- | 1:1 lessons with Rype tutors; requires separate Rype account ([Grokipedia](https://grokipedia.com/page/Jumpspeak)) |

Note: Pricing structure has been criticized in reviews for being confusing (upsells after signup, unclear tier differences) ([MezzoGuild](https://www.mezzoguild.com/jumpspeak-review/)).

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays ~$8.25/mo (annual) for 1,000+ conversation lessons with AI Tutor access.
- **A serious learner** pays ~$8.25/mo (annual) or $249 lifetime for permanent access.
- **A learner wanting human tutoring** pays ~$8.25/mo for Jumpspeak + $79-199/mo for Rype live tutoring (separate subscription, separate account) = $87-207/month total.
- **An enterprise buyer** cannot purchase Jumpspeak. No admin tools, no team management.

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | Jumpspeak | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Free tier | 7-day trial | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry paid | ~$8.25/mo (annual) | $19/mo (Plus) |
| Premium | $249 lifetime | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | Not available | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Included in subscription | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | $79-199/mo via Rype (separate) | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Languages | 20 (7 full, 13 AI-only) | Multiple (TBD count) |
| Business content | Some business scenarios | Full business programs |
| CEFR certification | None | Included |
| Lesson structure | Listen-Write-Speak-Conversation | TBD (Sep 2026 MVP) |

**Key Insight:** Jumpspeak's annual pricing ($99/yr) seems affordable, but adding live tutoring via Rype ($79-199/mo) quickly exceeds Berlitz Pro ($49/mo). Berlitz bundles AI practice (free), AI tutor sessions, and human coaching in a single platform with a single account. Jumpspeak's fragmented Rype/Jumpspeak split creates a worse user experience at a comparable or higher total cost.
