# Preply - Pricing Analysis

## Pricing Model

Marketplace/per-session with a 28-day recurring billing cycle [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/). No subscription for content access; learners pay per lesson directly to tutors. Preply takes a commission on each lesson (100% of the first lesson, 18-33% on subsequent lessons) [(Sacra)](https://sacra.com/c/preply/). Preply Business uses per-seat corporate pricing [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training).

## Consumer Pricing

| Tier | Monthly Price | Annual Price (per month) | What's Included |
|------|--------------|-------------------------|-----------------|
| Platform access | $0 | $0 | Browse tutors, AI matching [(Preply About)](https://preply.com/en/about-us) |
| Tutor lessons | $3-80/session | N/A | 1:1 live video tutoring; price set by individual tutor [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/) |
| Average lesson cost | ~$10-25/session | N/A | Mid-range tutor for common languages (English avg $10-15/hr) [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/) |
| Premium tutor | $40-80/session | N/A | Experienced/specialized tutors (business, exam prep) [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/) |

Rates vary significantly by language. Examples: English $10-15/hr avg, Spanish $8-12/hr avg, French $20-25/hr avg, German $25-35/hr avg [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/). Average tutor hourly rate across the platform is $18.30 [(Sacra)](https://sacra.com/c/preply/).

Typical monthly spend: 4-8 lessons = $40-200/month depending on tutor and frequency [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/). Payments are charged upfront for each 28-day cycle; only 1 week of unused hours rolls over [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/).

## Enterprise/B2B Pricing

- **Preply Business:** Custom pricing per seat [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training)
- **Estimated per-seat cost:** $50-150/user/month depending on lesson frequency and tutor tier (Medium confidence estimate)
- **Minimum seats:** Varies; typically 5+ [(Software Finder)](https://softwarefinder.com/lms/preply-business)
- **Features included:** Admin dashboard, progress reports, dedicated CSM, tutor matching, SSO [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training) [(Software Finder)](https://softwarefinder.com/lms/preply-business)
- **Volume discounts:** Available for larger teams [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training)

## Government/Institutional Pricing

Not specifically marketed to government [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training). Educational institution partnerships available.

## What the End User Actually Pays

- **A casual learner** pays ~$10-25/session for a 1:1 tutor lesson [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/). At 4 lessons/month, that is $40-100/month, significantly more than app-based competitors.
- **A serious learner** pays ~$160-400/month for 8-16 sessions with a quality tutor (at $20/hr: $160 for 8 sessions, $320 for 16 sessions per 28-day cycle) [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/).
- **An enterprise buyer** pays $50-150/user/month for corporate language training with admin tools and reporting (Medium confidence estimate based on marketplace rates and enterprise features).

## Comparison to Berlitz (Target)

| Dimension | Preply | Berlitz (Target) |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Free tier | Browse + trial lesson [(Preply About)](https://preply.com/en/about-us) | Unlimited AI practice + 1 demo class |
| Entry cost (4 lessons/mo) | ~$40-100/mo [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/) | $19/mo (Plus, 2 AI tutor sessions) |
| Mid-tier (8 lessons/mo) | ~$80-200/mo [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/) | $49/mo (Pro, 6 credits incl. human) |
| Premium (16 lessons/mo) | ~$160-400/mo [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/) | $99/mo (Premium, 16 credits) |
| Enterprise | $50-150/user/month (estimated) | Custom (admin-managed pools) |
| AI practice cost | Not available separately [(Preply Blog, Aug 2025)](https://preply.com/en/blog/preply-announces-new-ai-powered-features-to-guide-the-future-of-personalized-learning-in-a-human-ai-world/) | Free on all tiers |
| Human teacher cost | $3-80/session (marketplace) [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/) | 1-2 credits/session ($5-10) |
| Tutor quality | Variable (marketplace) [(Sacra)](https://sacra.com/c/preply/) | Curated (97.8% satisfaction) |
| Business content | Tutor-dependent [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training) | Structured business programs |
| CEFR certification | Not available [(Preply About)](https://preply.com/en/about-us) | Included |

**Key Insight:** Preply is the most expensive option for regular human tutoring ($40-400/mo depending on frequency and tutor tier) because every session is paid [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/). Berlitz's credit system offers human sessions at a fraction of the cost ($5-10/session) while bundling unlimited AI practice. The value gap widens at higher usage: a serious learner paying $200+/mo on Preply gets the equivalent of Berlitz Pro ($49/mo) with better instructor quality assurance.

## Methodology

Pricing research conducted June 2026 using 6+ web searches targeting tutor rates, commission structure, enterprise pricing, and cost comparisons. Primary sources (Preply website, Help Center) were cross-referenced with aggregator sites (Brighterly, Sacra). Consumer pricing is well-documented across multiple sources. Enterprise pricing is estimated (Low confidence) as Preply Business uses custom quotes. Rate ranges were updated from the original to reflect lower-end rates ($3/hr) found in current research. All Berlitz comparison figures are internal targets, not publicly sourced.
