## Product Summary

Online tutoring marketplace connecting learners with human tutors, augmented by AI-powered co-pilot tools for tutors [(Preply Blog, Jan 2026)](https://preply.com/en/blog/preply-raises-150-million-to-shape-the-future-of-education-through-human-led-ai-enhanced-learning/). Preply Business offers corporate language training with 1:1 tutoring and admin reporting [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training).

**Target Learner:** Adults who want personalized 1:1 tutoring with a human teacher, supplemented by AI tools. Business professionals and corporate teams [(Preply Blog, Jan 2026)](https://preply.com/en/blog/preply-raises-150-million-to-shape-the-future-of-education-through-human-led-ai-enhanced-learning/).

## Avatar/Visual AI Analysis

Preply uses human tutors, not avatars [(Preply About)](https://preply.com/en/about-us):

- **Implementation:** Real human tutors via live video calls. No AI avatar.
- **Interaction model:** Learner books a session with a tutor; sessions happen live via the Preply platform.
- **Comparison to Berlitz:** Preply's model is closest to Berlitz's human-teacher side but lacks the AI avatar/practice layer. Preply is adding AI co-pilot tools to support tutors [(Preply Blog, Aug 2025)](https://preply.com/en/blog/preply-announces-new-ai-powered-features-to-guide-the-future-of-personalized-learning-in-a-human-ai-world/), while Berlitz is building AI-first with human teacher escalation.

- SSO and LMS integration available for enterprise [(Software Finder)](https://softwarefinder.com/lms/preply-business)

## Weaknesses vs Berlitz

- Inconsistent tutor quality: marketplace model means variable experience [(Sacra)](https://sacra.com/c/preply/)
- No owned curriculum or structured learning paths [(Sacra)](https://sacra.com/c/preply/)
- No CEFR certification or formal assessment (despite claiming CEFR-level progress in studies) [(Preply About)](https://preply.com/en/about-us)
- AI capabilities are tutor-support tools, not learner-facing AI practice [(Preply Blog, Aug 2025)](https://preply.com/en/blog/preply-announces-new-ai-powered-features-to-guide-the-future-of-personalized-learning-in-a-human-ai-world/)
- No AI avatar or unlimited AI conversation practice [(Preply About)](https://preply.com/en/about-us)
- No business content programs (skills, culture, compliance) [(Preply Business page)](https://preply.com/en/business-language-training)
- Higher per-session cost for quality tutors vs Berlitz's credit system [(Brighterly, 2026)](https://brighterly.com/blog/preply-cost/)
- No offline mode or self-study content [(Preply About)](https://preply.com/en/about-us)
- Revenue per learner is limited by marketplace commission model (100% on first lesson, 18-33% thereafter) [(Sacra)](https://sacra.com/c/preply/)

## Berlitz Positioning vs This Competitor

Preply has the strongest human-tutoring marketplace but relies on freelance tutors with inconsistent quality and no owned curriculum [(Sacra)](https://sacra.com/c/preply/). Berlitz delivers the best of both worlds: unlimited AI practice for daily fluency building plus certified Berlitz instructors (97.8% satisfaction, 5-year avg tenure) for structured coaching. For enterprise buyers, Berlitz offers ROI dashboards, CEFR certification, and business skills programs that Preply's marketplace model cannot match.

## Methodology

Research conducted June 2026 using 6+ web searches with varied queries covering company financials, product features, pricing, AI capabilities, enterprise offerings, and recent news. Primary sources (Preply's own blog and website) were cross-referenced with secondary sources (Tech.eu, Sacra, Fueler, Vestbee, Brighterly) to verify claims. Where sources conflict (notably revenue estimates), ranges are provided with both sources cited. Confidence ratings follow the standard: High = 2+ independent sources or primary source, Medium = 1 credible source, Low = unverified or estimated.
