# Berlitz Platform Vision — AI-Native Workforce Communication

**Source:** Board Update — Product/Technology (June 2, 2026)
**Audience:** Board, P&T leadership
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-04

## The Vision

Berlitz operates as an **AI-native workforce communication platform with usage-based monetization and enterprise learning intelligence** — not an education services company. Competitors can copy features faster than they can copy operational data, so the durable advantage is the data and operational moat created by connecting learning behavior directly to revenue and outcomes.

## Product Strategy: Build Products That Drive Outcomes

The product organization shifts from feature delivery, stakeholder requests, and project-based thinking toward **measurable outcomes, platform thinking, AI-native experiences, and monetization-aware product development.**

Guiding principles:

- Every initiative connects to business outcomes, learner outcomes, and operational efficiency.
- Product strategy is driven by revenue intelligence and operational telemetry.
- AI and human-in-the-loop workflows are designed together.

Targeted outcomes: reduced time-to-market, a higher-ROI roadmap, improved retention and expansion, and better engagement with the product.

## The Dual Flywheel

Two reinforcing flywheels compound into a defensible platform:

- **Flywheel 1 — B2C / Self-Serve (Volume & Speed):** millions of learners practice daily and make mistakes, generating learning data. AI diagnostics measure baseline level, micro-tracking captures every error and hesitation, and gamified milestones unlock levels based on proven mastery. The data asset created is a massive dataset on how humans learn specific skills, enabling hyper-personalized AI tutors for any subject.
- **Flywheel 2 — B2B / Enterprise (Depth & Validation):** enterprises and HR buyers buy programs for teams and demand outcomes and ROI, generating contracts and revenue. Pre/post assessments measure skill gaps, manager feedback loops integrate with HRIS (Kombo), certification provides verifiable credentials, and ROI dashboards show "time to fluency" and business impact.

The two connect: **better experience → more users → more data → smarter AI → better enterprise outcomes → enterprise demand → more revenue → more investment → better product.** The core engine is a hybrid AI platform combining AI models and personalization, curriculum and content, human + AI coaching, and assessment and certification — producing fluency, personalization, certification, and ROI.

## AI & "Data as Asset" Strategy

The operating principle across all AI is **"AI Suggests. Humans Decide."** — maintaining quality, ethics, and brand through human-in-the-loop guardrails and governance. AI is organized into four layers:

- **Infrastructure AI** — take unified raw data to model. This is the foundation and the moat.
- **Operational AI** — smart scheduling, pricing & yield, billing & fraud. Handles the "boring stuff" so humans focus on high-value tasks; this is where cost reduction, scale, and efficiency come from.
- **Insight AI** — churn prediction, reports/dashboards, content improvement. Drives ROI for B2B clients and improves churn and retention.
- **Product AI** — level assessment, speech (STT/TTS) and writing, adaptive roleplay/simulations. AI for practice, humans for coaching (personalized learning).
- **Generative AI** — curriculum drafting, personalized copy, support chatbots: curriculum and marketing at scale, with human review.

## Technology Strategy: A Scalable Learning Platform

The platform moves **from a monolithic legacy system to modular platform primitives with clear system responsibilities**, organized in layers:

- **Experience & Engagement Layer** — how users interact with Berlitz: self-serve learning apps, B2B enterprise admin portals, and human + AI learning (scheduling, diagnostics, certifications). Built with AI agents in React Native/Flutter.
- **Commercial & Operational Intelligence Layer** — a learning revenue platform (credits, entitlements, usage metering, monetization), operational intelligence (teacher allocation, AI/human routing, scheduling optimization), and enterprise commerce (contracts, renewals). Built on Metronome, Stripe, NetSuite.
- **Data, AI & Platform Infrastructure Layer** — a data platform (telemetry, event streaming, analytics, executive intelligence), AI infrastructure (LLM, recommendation, personalization, speech & adaptive learning), and platform engineering (cloud infrastructure, observability, CI/CD, security, scalability, reliability).

The CRM layer is Salesforce or HubSpot with no CPQ. Intended outcomes: personalization for learners, optimization/yield, reduced operational overhead, and lower complexity with faster delivery.

## Talent & Culture: An AI-Native Product/Tech Org

The org moves from siloed teams and feature factories to **small senior teams using AI-assisted execution, systems thinking, and cross-functional delivery.** Three functions — Product, Design, Engineering — embed AI in day-to-day work, ship nothing without an outcome, optimize for learning velocity, automate first (no dedicated QA role), and operate with "no HiPPOs." AI-native engineering spans AI agents in coding/review/routing, fast and deterministic AI-native CI, AI-and-human-readable knowledge docs, and fully reversible, auditable GitOps/CD.

## Future State: To Build Legacy or To Pivot

The strategic choice contrasts staying on Salesforce/STAR with moving to a dedicated revenue platform:

- **Salesforce/STAR** manages contracts and operations but carries high TCO ($8–9M/year), is strong for CRM but weak operationally, locks business logic into Salesforce (lowering product agility), and is optimized for contract negotiation rather than monetization.
- **Revenue Platform** monetizes and optimizes consumption in real time, with lower long-term cost via clean separation of CRM, operations, monetization, payments, and finance. It enables flexible credits, AI/human usage pricing, enterprise pools, and dynamic packaging — and creates a strategic data/AI moat by connecting learning behavior directly to revenue and enterprise outcomes.

## Why It Matters: Valuation & Narrative

The transformation creates a scalable monetization and operating model that changes how Berlitz is valued:

- **Financial transformation:** improves gross margin narrative, creates predictable recurring revenue, and improves scalability perception.
- **Platform transformation:** moves from a services to a platform company; cleanly separates CRM, monetization, payments, and finance; reduces Salesforce customization and consulting dependence; and builds a data & AI moat/defensibility.
- **Exit & valuation narrative:** shifts the story from education services to AI-native platform, making Berlitz more attractive to SaaS, HR-tech, and AI buyers, and reduces technical diligence risk at exit.
- **Narrative shift:** "We operate an AI-native workforce communication platform with usage-based monetization and enterprise learning intelligence."
