# Berlitz - Jobs to Be Done & User Personas

**Source:** Board Update — Product/Technology (June 2, 2026)
**Last Updated:** 2026-06-04

# Jobs to Be Done

## JTBD 1: Reach a real communication outcome
"When I want to actually communicate in another language or skill, I want measurable progress through unlimited AI practice plus human coaching, so I reach proven fluency instead of just finishing a course."

## JTBD 2: Get job-ready business communication
"When I need business English or a professional skill for my career, I want targeted role-play, tutoring, and human sessions, so I can perform at work and demonstrate it credibly."

## JTBD 3: Prove L&D ROI to the business (HR buyer)
"When I buy a learning program for my teams, I want pre/post assessments, manager feedback loops, certification, and ROI dashboards, so I can show business impact and justify the investment."

## JTBD 4: Manage and optimize team learning budget (enterprise admin)
"When I manage learning across an organization, I want to allocate credits per user/team with real-time usage and visibility, so I can control budget centrally and optimize spend across the org."

## JTBD 5: Teach effectively with less manual work (instructor)
"When I deliver a session, I want learner history, AI-assisted prep, and frictionless scheduling, so I spend time coaching instead of on manual tasks — and my observations aren't lost."

# User Personas

## Hobbyist - "Hobbyist Hana"

- **Segment:** B2P self-serve, casual learner
- **Goals:** Practice consistently, build a habit, improve gradually without big commitment
- **Frustrations:** Most apps feel shallow; no real human feedback; paying feels risky before seeing value
- **Berlitz usage:** Unlimited free AI practice plus 2 AI tutor sessions/month; generates learning data for the platform
- **Tier:** Plus ($19/mo) — break-even + data

## Professional - "Professional Pedro"

- **Segment:** B2P self-serve, working professional
- **Goals:** Improve practical communication for work, see measurable progress, mix AI and human practice
- **Frustrations:** Limited time, needs efficient sessions, wants outcomes not just lessons
- **Berlitz usage:** 6 credits/month, unlimited AI practice, 4 AI tutor + 2 human sessions
- **Tier:** Pro ($49/mo) — core profit driver

## Career-changer - "Career Carla"

- **Segment:** B2P self-pay, often funded for/by work (B2B self-pay)
- **Goals:** Business English or a specific professional skill (role play + human), credential to show employers
- **Frustrations:** Generic language apps don't cover business contexts; needs proof of skill acquisition
- **Berlitz usage:** 6 credits/month, unlimited AI practice, 4 AI tutor + 1 human session, 1 free skill module
- **Tier:** Career ($39/mo)

## Executive - "Executive Elena"

- **Segment:** B2P premium / sponsored by enterprise
- **Goals:** High-touch, high-quality coaching with maximum human access; relocation/culture and skills programs
- **Frustrations:** Needs flexibility, premium quality, and fast outcomes; low tolerance for friction
- **Berlitz usage:** 16 credits/month, unlimited AI practice, 8 AI tutor + 4 EU human sessions; skills/culture bundles
- **Tier:** Premium ($99/mo) — high LTV & margin

## Enterprise HR Buyer - "Buyer Ben"

- **Segment:** B2B enterprise / mid-market decision maker
- **Goals:** Prove ROI, track skill gaps via pre/post assessments, integrate with HRIS (Kombo), certify outcomes, expand into Skills/Culture programs
- **Frustrations:** Hard to demonstrate L&D impact; needs verifiable proof of value and "time to fluency" metrics
- **Berlitz usage:** Admin-managed credit pools, ROI dashboards, manager feedback loops, certification; NRR target ~120%
- **Tier:** Enterprise (custom) — ROI-driven

## Instructor - "Instructor Ingrid"

- **Segment:** Supply side — the platform's strategic moat
- **Goals:** Coach effectively, see learner context, minimize manual prep, schedule with low friction, get paid for prep
- **Frustrations:** 0% learner data shared from sales; no first-5-minute personalization; 10–15 min/session lost to manual tasks; observations lost between sessions; static materials, unpaid prep
- **Berlitz usage:** Learner info persisted across sessions, AI-assisted lesson prep, self-study activity feed, closed loop with AI, dynamic personalized curriculum
- **Target metrics:** 60–65% utilization (vs. 45.7%), instructor NPS >70, prep-time reduction >40%
